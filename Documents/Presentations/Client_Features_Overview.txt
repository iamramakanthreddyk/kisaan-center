KisaanCenter: User Features & Pain Points Solved

Who Uses the System?
- Shop Owners: Manage stalls, track sales, payments, and expenses.
- Farmers: Deliver flowers, track stock, view sales, and request payments.
- Buyers: Purchase flowers, pay immediately or on credit, receive receipts.
- Employees: Assist with operations, record transactions, manage inventory.

Key Features for Each User

Shop Owner Features
- Dashboard Overview: See all flower stocks, sales, payments, and expenses in one place.
- Automated Record-Keeping: No more paper ledgers—every delivery, sale, and payment is digitally tracked.
- Commission Management: System calculates commissions automatically for each transaction.
- Payment Tracking: Instantly see which buyers have paid, which are on credit, and which farmers are owed money.
- Expense Management: Record and categorize shop expenses (wages, rent, utilities, etc.).
- Audit Logs: Every change (stock, payment, transaction) is logged for transparency and dispute resolution.
- Reporting: Generate sales, payment, and expense reports for any period.
- Multi-User Access: Owners can add employees to help manage operations securely.

Farmer Features
- Stock Tracking: View all flower deliveries and current stock at each shop.
- Sales Overview: See how much of each flower type was sold, and for what price.
- Payment Requests: Request payment for delivered flowers, track pending and completed payments.
- Commission Transparency: See exactly how much commission was deducted per sale.
- Credit Management: Know if buyers have paid or if payment is pending.

Buyer Features
- Product Browsing: View available flowers and prices at each shop.
- Flexible Payments: Pay immediately or request credit (if allowed by shop).
- Receipts: Receive digital receipts for every purchase.
- Credit Tracking: See outstanding payments and due dates.

Employee Features
- Transaction Entry: Record sales, deliveries, and payments.
- Inventory Management: Update stock levels as flowers are delivered or sold.
- Expense Entry: Log shop expenses as they occur.

Pain Points Solved
- Manual Errors & Time Waste: Automated records eliminate calculation mistakes and save hours of manual work.
- Payment Disputes: Transparent logs and reports make it easy to resolve disagreements over payments or commissions.
- Credit Risks: Track buyer credits and automate reminders, reducing defaults and missed payments.
- Fragmented Information: All data (stock, sales, payments, expenses) is centralized and accessible anytime.
- Seasonal Overload: System scales to handle high transaction volumes during festivals or peak seasons.
- Regulatory Compliance: Detailed audit logs and reports help with tax filing and regulatory checks.
- Multi-Shop Confusion: Farmers and owners can see all activity across multiple shops in one dashboard.

Example User Journey (Owner)
1. Morning: Owner logs in, sees dashboard with all flower stocks and pending payments.
2. During the Day: Employees record deliveries from farmers and sales to buyers.
3. End of Week/Month: Owner reviews sales, checks which farmers are owed money, and processes payments with one click.
4. Anytime: Owner generates reports for sales, payments, and expenses; reviews audit logs if a dispute arises.

This system transforms the flower market from manual, error-prone processes to a modern, transparent, and efficient business—making life easier for owners, farmers, buyers, and employees.

KisaanCenter Owner Workflow Diagram (Flowers Only)

[Farmer delivers flowers] --> [Shop records flower stock]
[Shop records flower stock] --> [Flowers available for sale]
[Flowers available for sale] --> [Buyer purchases flowers]
[Buyer purchases flowers] --> [Transaction recorded]
[Transaction recorded] --> [Shop receives payment]
[Shop receives payment] --> [Commission calculated]
[Commission calculated] --> [Farmer requests payment]
[Farmer requests payment] --> [Shop pays farmer]
[Shop pays farmer] --> [Audit log]
